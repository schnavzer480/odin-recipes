Jok

Jok, a popular Thai dish, is a savory rice porridge known for its comforting and flavorful qualities. Commonly enjoyed as a breakfast or light meal, Jok features tender rice cooked to a creamy consistency and is typically garnished with a variety of savory toppings such as minced meat, ginger, and green onions.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Chicken or pork (minced or thinly sliced)
  • Chicken or pork broth
  • Ginger (minced or thinly sliced)
  • Garlic (minced)
  • Soy sauce
  • Fish sauce
  • White pepper
  • Salt
  • Toppings (optional): Green onions, cilantro, crispy fried garlic, a dash of sesame oil

Steps

Cooking

  1. Cook rice in chicken or pork broth until soft and creamy.
  2. Saut√© minced ginger and garlic until fragrant in a separate pan.
  3. Add minced or thinly sliced chicken or pork to the aromatic mixture; cook until fully done.
  4. Season the meat with soy sauce, fish sauce, white pepper, and salt. Adjust to taste.
  5. Combine the seasoned meat mixture with the rice porridge; let it simmer.
  6. Optionally, garnish with green onions, cilantro, crispy fried garlic, and a dash of sesame oil.
  7. Ladle the Jok into bowls and serve hot.
